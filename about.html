About Us

OurDaniel is an international fan site dedicated to Kang Daniel, a South Korean singer, songwriter, dancer, commercial model, and CEO of one-man agency KONNECT Entertainment. He is best known as the first place winner of survival show Produce 101 Season 2 and former member of project group Wanna One.

This website is a collaborative effort of a small group of noona fans who love and support Daniel since the first episode of PD101. We created this
website to compile all the Daniel-related information and to use it as a platform for promotion and community for all of his fans around the world.

Open since June 16, 2018.
